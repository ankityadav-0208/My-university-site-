About Our University

GJUST Hisar was established on October 20, 1995, named after Guru Jambheshwar Ji Maharaj, a saint-environmentalist. The university is spread over 372 acres of lush green campus and is known for its focus on Science, Technology, Engineering, Pharmacy, and Management studies.

Management

PCI approved Pharmaceutical sciences focusing on research and development.

Pure Sciences

Advanced studies in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
